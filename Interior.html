Black Nappo Leather

This is a top-tier Mercedes-Benz upholstery option featuring exceptionally soft, high-quality, and finely grained leather, often used in AMG and luxury models. It is highly customizable, with options for special colors (e.g., Porcelain, Saddle Brown, Classic Red), stitching, and diamond-quilted patterns, enhancing both comfort and visual appeal. These materials are designed for durability, but require care to prevent staining and fading.


MB-Tex

This is a premium, synthetic vinyl upholstery material engineered by Mercedes-Benz to emulate the look and feel of leather while providing superior durability and lower maintenance. It is highly resistant to cracking, fading, and staining, making it ideal for long-term wear. It is commonly used as standard seating material across many Mercedes-Benz models.

White-Leather

This a durable, flexible material created by tanning animal hides and skins (commonly cattle, sheep, goat) to prevent decay. Used for over 7,000 years, it is manufactured into products like our well-seated cars.
